Advantages of Eco-Grip® Restaurant Flooring

Eco-Grip® presents a plethora of advantages that set it apart from conventional flooring choices. When it comes to meeting the specific needs of restaurant environments, Eco-Grip® emerges as the safer, more convenient, and economically sound choice, making it the ultimate solution for your establishment's flooring requirements.

Effortless Maintenance

Eco-Grip® boasts a waterproof, chemical-resistant, and grease-resistant nature. Its non-porous surface simplifies cleaning tasks, making it the perfect fit for bustling restaurant kitchens. With an integrated micro biocide, Eco-Grip® actively combats the growth of bacteria, mold, and mildew, further enhancing hygiene levels in your establishment and potentially improving your sanitation rating.

Cost-Effective Investment

Eco-Grip® emerges as a cost-effective solution, standing in direct competition with traditional flooring options like quarry tile and poured floors. Furthermore, Eco-Grip® eliminates the need for periodic re-grouting of tiles and the use of specialized anti-slip mats, resulting in significant long-term cost savings. Backed by a 5-year limited manufacturer's warranty, Eco-Grip® ensures not only safety and convenience but also a financially sound investment that can yield savings for years to come.
